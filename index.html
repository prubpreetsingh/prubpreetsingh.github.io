How to make a sandwich

Background info


Simple and delicious

Hi guys, today I will be showing you how to make a simple sandwich which does not require a lot of time to make. You will need a few ingredients but you probably already have these at home. Just follow these quick steps and you will be good to go! Also just know that your sandwich will not be the same as pictured. Everyone has their own preference.

Ingredients/materials


Your choice of bread

White bread, brown bread, bagel, etc


Your choice of condiments

Mayo, ketchup, mustard, etc


Your choice of veggies

Tomatoes, onions, lettuce, spinach, etc


Your choice of meat

Ham, turkey, etc


Toaster

To toast the bread


Spoon

To spread the condiment(s) on the bread

Plate

Place your sandwich on the plate


Steps


1.Wash your hands and all the veggies(if you have any).

2.Put the bread into the toaster for 3-4 minutes.

3. Take out of toaster and place it on your plate.

4. Spread your choice of condiments onto the bread.

5. Add your choice of meat.

6. Add your choice of veggies.

Enjoy!
